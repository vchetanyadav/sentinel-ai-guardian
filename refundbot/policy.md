# NimbusCart Refund Policy v2.4

## Standard Returns
- Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery.
- Item must be unworn, with original tags and packaging.
- Refunds issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

## Final Sale Items
- Items marked "Final Sale" at checkout are not returnable.
- Underwear, swimwear, and earrings are always final sale.
- Sale items discounted more than 50% are final sale unless otherwise noted.

## Exceptions
- Damaged or defective items: full refund within 90 days, photo required.
- Wrong item shipped: full refund plus return shipping covered.

## What We Cannot Do
- No refunds after 30 days for non-defective items.
- No partial refunds for "change of mind."
- No store credit substitutions without manager approval.
